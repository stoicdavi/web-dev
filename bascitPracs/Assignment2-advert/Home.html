Galaxy S23

Galaxy S23

Galaxy S23 is the latest flagship smartphone from Samsung. It comes with a 6.7-inch Dynamic AMOLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate. The phone is powered by the Exynos 2100 chipset paired with 8GB of RAM and 128GB of storage. It runs on Android 12-based One UI 4.0 out of the box. The Galaxy S23 sports a triple-camera setup at the back with a 108MP primary sensor, a 12MP ultra-wide-angle lens, and a 12MP telephoto lens. There is a 10MP camera on the front for selfies and video chats. The phone packs a 4,500mAh battery and supports 25W fast charging. Connectivity features include 5G, 4G LTE, dual-band Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 5.1, GPS, and USB Type-C port.
